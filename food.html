Mon's _Fav_Foods_

These are some of my favorite breakfast, lunch, and dinner foods that I reccommend to anyone interested in trying something new!

Back

This is the type of breakfast that happens in my house only once in awhile but when it does occur I am the happiest! The mouth-watering platter consists of fried plantains, refried beans, scrambled eggs with tomato, cheese,cream, tortillas, and even better if it includes mexican chorizos.

My brother introduced me to this jamaican meal and it tasted great! There is jerk chicken, rice with beans, plantains, and salad.

My brother and dad took me to our local vietnamese restaurant where I ate pho and vermicelli! This is the vermicelli with grilled chicken and it is tasted awesome and it was also healthy!

I also really like pho, which has an interesting almost sweet flavor.

Drunken noodle is a spicy noodle dish that all spice and noodle lovers should try for a tasty meal!

A nice kabob is something that everyone should have at least once in their life! The tandoor bread was so good too!

Tacos are currently my favorite food. I especially love the tacos from Uncle Julio's!
